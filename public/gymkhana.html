In Trouble at IIT? Reach Out.

Student & Peer Support

For academic stress, personal issues, or mediation, reach out to student bodies.

  • Student Mentor: Your first point of contact for personal guidance.
  • Gymkhana President: Contact for issues related to student activities/policy. (president@gymkhana.iiti.ac.in)
  • Council Heads: For domain-specific help (Cultural, Tech, Sports, etc.)

Direct Access to Management

For serious matters requiring intervention from high-level institutional authorities.

  • Dean of Student Affairs (DoSA): dosa@iiti.ac.in
  • Director's Office: For highest level escalation.
  • Registrar's Office: For administrative support.
